Lunch

Attendees are expected to make their own plans for lunch. Although the area nearby is busy during the week, it's quiet at the weekend and many places are closed.

However, we expect the following places to be open (listed roughly in order of proximity):

  • Co-op, Fenchurch Street (OSM, Google Maps)
  • Tesco Express, Houndsditch (OSM, Google Maps)
  • Eat, Aldgate High Street (OSM, Google Maps)
  • Subway, Aldgate High Street (OSM, Google Maps)
  • Peter's Cafe, Aldgate High Street (OSM, Google Maps)
  • Starbucks, Minories (OSM, Google Maps)
  • Pizza Union, Mansell Street (OSM, Google Maps)
  • Sainsbury's, Mansell Street (OSM, Google Maps)
  • Pret, Byward Street (OSM, Google Maps)
  • Wrapchic, Byward Street (OSM, Google Maps)

There are also quite a few pubs within walking distance that do lunch, including the Draft House (OSM, Google Maps) where we've met for the Friday social event.

Kahaila (OSM) is very near the venue, has a lovely outdoor space and serves cake and maybe sandwiches.

If you know the area, we welcome your suggestions. Please submit a pull request against this file.
